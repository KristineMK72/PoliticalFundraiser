About the Campaign

Meet Bernie Penner

I am presently living just south of Brainerd in Long Lake Township. My wife Carol and I have been married for 57 years. We have two children; Russ Penner who has one son and two daughters and Lisa Lenz who has two sons giving us five grandchildren and a great-grandchild who will be 2 in Dec. I was raised in Carlton Minnesota which is the gateway to Jay Cooke State Park. We lived on a 40 Acre Hobby Farm with hundreds of acres of hunting land all around. After High School I enrolled at the University of Minnesota, Duluth Campus. The Vietnam war came along and interrupted my life as a new husband, father of son and a daughter on the way. I enlisted in the Navy, did my tour in Vietnam, earned the Combat Action Ribbon and finished out my 4-year enlistment in California By 1978 I had finished my undergrad in Biology and General Science and became a licensed Minnesota Teacher. My career took a turn to Agriculture when I was selected by the USDA-Farm Service Agency for a position as the County Executive Director of the combined office for Cass and Crow Wing County. After retiring from the USDA, I earned Masters degrees at St Cloud State and began working as a Clinical Family therapist in the Brainerd Lakes Area retiring from that position after 9 yrs. After nearly 40 years of working in careers that prevented me from being proactive in partisan politics, I became fully active with the Trump Campaign in the spring of 2024. Having directed programs passed by Congress for 4 decades I find it exhilarating and a challenge to help shape those programs by serving as your State Representative for House District 10A.

Campaign Priorities

  • Advocate for lower taxes and reduced government spending.
  • Support Second Amendment rights and oppose unnecessary gun control measures.
  • Promote policies that encourage job growth and economic development in our district.
  • Enhance public safety and support our law enforcement agencies.
  • Protect our community values and ensure quality education for our children.
